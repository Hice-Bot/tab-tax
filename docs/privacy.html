Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Overview

Tab Tax ("the Extension") is a browser extension for Google Chrome. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles information. The short version: Tab Tax does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

Data Collection

Tab Tax collects no personal data. The Extension does not:

  • Collect, store, or transmit browsing history
  • Track which websites you visit
  • Collect any personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Use cookies or any tracking technologies
  • Send any data to external servers or third parties
  • Include any analytics or telemetry

Local Storage

The Extension stores a small list of user-configured website rules (e.g., sites you choose to "tax") using Chrome's built-in local storage API. This data never leaves your device. It is only used to power the Extension's core functionality and is fully under your control — you can remove entries at any time from the Extension's popup, or uninstall the Extension to clear everything.

Permissions

Tab Tax requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — to save your list of taxed sites locally on your device.
  • activeTab — to read the URL of the current tab to check if it matches your list.
  • scripting — to display the mini-game challenge on matching pages.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit any data.

Third Parties

Tab Tax does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs. No data is shared with anyone.
